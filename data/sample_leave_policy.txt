Company Leave Policy

All full-time employees are eligible for 15 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

Employees become eligible for paid leave only after completing 6 months of continuous service with the company. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 working days in advance through the HR portal, except in emergencies.

Sick leave is provided separately, with up to 10 days per year. Sick leave does not require advance notice but must be reported to the manager on the same day, with a medical certificate required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

Unused annual leave can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any leave beyond this cap is forfeited at the end of the calendar year.

Maternity leave is granted for 26 weeks as per company policy, and paternity leave is granted for 2 weeks, both fully paid.
